Welcome to Tent City ATL

The tentCity app is a practical preview and intro to camp life and responsibilities.

What is Tent City ATL?

Tent City ATL is a resistance protest camp with the intention of obtaining from GSU and Carter Development, a legally binding community benefits agreement for the surrounding communities.

What can I do to help?

Thanks for asking! Keeping a camp like this takes a community. Find the camp coordinator on duty, sign into our guest book and ask how you can help.
